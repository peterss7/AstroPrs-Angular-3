Astro Home

Astro PRS was conceived of, designed, and built by the work of one man named Steven Peterson.

Steven has always had his eyes toward the sky as space travel is the final frontier for mankind. Unable to achieve his dream as the first astronaut to Mars due to a debilitating fear of heights, he has worked tirelessly to bring space to Earth. In his mission to provide otherworldly services to the masses, he has tested the limits of computer programming to create Astro PRS and bring information from around not just the United States, not just internaationally, but from all over the universe to create the largest and most comprehensive database ever seen.
